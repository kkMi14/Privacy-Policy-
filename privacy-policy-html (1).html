Privacy Policy for AgricultureBot

Effective Date: April 23, 2025

Introduction

AgricultureBot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and disclosed when you use our AgricultureBot mobile application ("App").

Information We Collect

AgricultureBot collects minimal information necessary for the functioning of the App, all of which is stored exclusively on your device:

  • User Preferences: Including notification settings and preferences
  • Selected Domains: Agricultural categories or topics you select for personalized content
  • App Usage Data: Basic information about how you use the App to improve functionality

How We Store Your Information

All information collected by AgricultureBot is:

  • Stored locally on your device only
  • Never transmitted to our servers or cloud storage
  • Not accessible to us or any third parties
  • Retained only as long as you have the App installed

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not share your data with any third parties. Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • No personal information is transmitted outside your device
  • No data is sold, rented, or shared with advertisers or analytics providers
  • No cloud backups of your data are created

Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

  • View Data: All stored information can be viewed in the App settings
  • Modify Data: Change your preferences and selections at any time through the App interface
  • Delete Data: You can delete all data by:
    • Clearing the App data through your device settings
    • Uninstalling the App from your device

Changes to Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy in the App and updating the "Effective Date" at the top.
